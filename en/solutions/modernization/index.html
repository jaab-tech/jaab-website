Modern COBOL

https://cobolcloud.io

EBS and CobolCloud combine decades of expertise in mainframe modernization with cutting-edge open-source technology, offering clients a proven path to transform their COBOL estates while controlling costs and accelerating innovation.

CobolCloud is the most advanced open-source COBOL solution to Host, Rethink and Operate your COBOL applications in the Cloud, Linux or Windows. This powerful platform enables organizations to modernize their COBOL applications without rewriting critical business logic.

Key Capabilities

  • Open-source COBOL compiler optimized for modern platforms
  • Support for major COBOL dialects with no source code modification
  • Full integration with cloud services (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Cost reduction through elimination of mainframe licensing
  • Modern development and deployment workflows
  • Enterprise-grade tools for production operations

Success Story: AFD (French Development Agency)

AFD, a public financial institution that implements the policy defined by the French government, chose CobolCloud to modernize its critical COBOL applications. This successful migration demonstrates the power of combining EBS expertise with CobolCloud technology to transform legacy systems while maintaining business continuity.
